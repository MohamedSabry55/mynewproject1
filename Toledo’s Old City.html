The old city of Toledo is a city in which Moorish, Gothic and Renaissance architecture is mixed in one place. In this city, many tourist attractions, especially historical, can be seen, from churches, monasteries, museums, historical buildings, and everything that any history lover desires.

Toledo is the capital of the province of Toledo and the Castile-La Mancha region in central Spain. It has a population of about 83,108 people. The city is located on an impenetrable height surrounded by deep valleys and deep cliffs.

Location:75 km south of the Spanish capital, Madrid
