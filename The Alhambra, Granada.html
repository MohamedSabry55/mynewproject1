The Alhambra is an ancient palace and fortress, and one of the most important monuments of poor Islamic architecture in Andalusia.

It was built by the founder of the state of Bani al-Ahmar “Al-Ghalib Billah” Abi Abdullah Muhammad I Muhammad ibn Yusuf ibn Muhammad ibn Ahmad ibn Nasr ibn al-Ahmar between 1238-1273 in the Kingdom of Granada during the second half of the tenth century AD.
