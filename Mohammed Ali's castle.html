The Mosque of Muhammad Ali or Alabaster Mosque or Alabaster Mosque is one of the famous archaeological mosques in Cairo. It was established by Muhammad Ali Pasha between the period from 1830 AD to 1848 AD in the Ottoman style, similar to the Sultan Ahmed Mosque in Istanbul

It is sometimes called the Alabaster or Alabaster Mosque due to the frequent use of this type of marble in the cladding of its walls.

Location:The Citadel, Al-Abagiyah, Qism Al-Khalifa, Cairo Governorate
