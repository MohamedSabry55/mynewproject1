The Tower of Pisa is located in Italy in the city of Pisa in the state of Tuscany. Its construction began in 1173 AD, and its construction lasted 199 years. It was known as the Leaning Tower of Pisa because of its tilt and deviation from the vertical plane. It consists of eight floors built of white marble in the Roman style, with a height of 56.2 meters and a staircase built into the walls consisting of 294 steps, (currently equipped with an elevator).
