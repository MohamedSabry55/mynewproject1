The Giza pyramid complex is an archaeological site on the Giza plateau, on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt.

It includes the three great pyramids (Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure), the Great Sphinx, several tombs, a workers' village and an industrial complex.

It is located in the Western Desert, about 9 km (5 mi) west of the Nile River in the old town of Giza, and about 13 km (8 mi) southwest of downtown Cairo.
