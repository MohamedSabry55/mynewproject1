Saint Catherine's Monastery is located in southern Sinai, Egypt, below Mount Catherine, the highest mountains in Egypt, near Mount Moses, in the city of Saint Catherine. It is said that it is the oldest monastery in the world.

It is considered a great tourist attraction, as it is visited by tourist groups from all over the world. It is a retreat, run by the head of the monastery, the Bishop of Sinai, who is not subject to the authority of any patriarch or a holy council, but has close relations with the Patriarch of Jerusalem.
