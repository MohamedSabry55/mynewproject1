Borghese Gallery (Italian: Galleria Borghese) is an art gallery located in Rome, Italy, located in the former residence of Villa Borghese Ponciana, a building that was formerly an integrated unit with its distinctive gardens surrounding it, which is nowadays completely independent and called by tourists Gardens Villa Borghese.

Scipione Borghese was an early patron of Jean Lorenzo Bernini and was an avid collector of the works of Caravaggio who are well represented in the collection called Born with a Basket of Fruit.
