Qaitbay Castle is located at the end of Pharos Island in the far west of Alexandria. It was built in the place of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria, which was destroyed in the year 702 AH following the devastating earthquake that occurred during the reign of Sultan Al-Nasir Muhammad ibn Qalawun. It was built in 884 AH.

The reason for his interest in Alexandria was the large number of direct threats to Egypt from the Ottoman Empire, which threatened the entire Arab region.
